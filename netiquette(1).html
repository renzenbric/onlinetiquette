Netiquette in Online Communication

Target Audience: Netiquette rules on this page were crafted for professionals, students, general public using online communication.

Tips

  • Chats Room Tips

    1. Identify yourself and your affiliations clearly.
    2. Create single subject messages.
    3. Stay on-topic and give pertinent, accurate, and cocise information
    4. Keep in mind your intended audience.

What is Netiquette?

Netiquette is defined as a set of rules for acceptable online behavior. It is a code of good behavior on the internet. This includes proper manners for sending email, conversing online, and so on. It means respecting other users’ views and displaying common courtesy when posting your views to online discussion groups.

Be polite, respectful, and thoughtful

Remember that we are all humans; be forgiving of other people’s mistakes.You may express robust disagreement with what someone says, but don't call them names, or threaten them with personal violence, or try to make them feel bad, or hurt their feelings.

Respect Others privacy

Do not disclose information about others without their permission. Do not share their photos, videos on social media even if you appear in the photos or videos; soem poeple like to keep their lives private.

Use proper language

When communicating on the internet, those rules must be followed regarding proper language: select appropriate style and tone of written expression,think about the level of formality you put in a message, use proper puntuation, grammar, syntax. and diction.
